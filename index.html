    Ciapre

    Ciapre - an easy on the eyes Sublime Text/TextMate color scheme.

    • https://github.com/vinhnx/Ciapre.tmTheme
    • https://sublime.wbond.net/packages/Ciapre%20Color%20Scheme
    Installation

    Just search for Ciapre in Sublime Text's Package Control Manager & install it.

    Then, in menu bar, browse to Preferences > Color Scheme and choose your desired one!

    Comes with two different colors

    Dark

    • Color Pallete #D9487D #262626 #BFB38F #D99A4E #BF4545
    Blue

    • Pallete #D9487D #383B73 #171C26 #BFB38F #A63333
